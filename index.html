Community Reach

The Mobile Health Unit serves communities throughout Tucson and surrounding areas by meeting people where they are—in neighborhoods, partner sites, and community events.

All data shown are aggregated and de-identified to protect participant privacy.

Community Health Snapshot

Why Screenings Matter

Early screenings can help people identify possible health concerns before they become emergencies. The Mobile Health Unit helps connect participants to follow-up care, education, and local resources.
